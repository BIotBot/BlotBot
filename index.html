A Twitch bot that moderates your chat for derogatory and toxic behavior. Offending messages are "blotted" out before they can reach critical mass.

Adopt the bot

The Big Picture

Twitch chat has a major harassment problem.

Toxic Frequency

Misogynistic, homophobic, and racial slurs are not rare occurences on Twitch chat

Gender Disparity

Women receive three times as many derogatory remarks than men in online gaming environments

Devalued Interaction

Twitch chat exists as a medium of communication between a streamer and their audience

A Closer Look

BlotBot is well equiped to solve torrents of Twitch chat harassment.

"If you come up with a list of banned words, people are pretty creative, that’s not going to stop it by itself."

Self-Learning

BlotBot learns what messages are toxic or offensive when moderators timeout or ban a user in real-time.

"They didn’t have enough moderators, they didn’t have the right ones, and they didn’t fully use the tools available to them."

Efficient

BlotBot helps moderators scale to the large flood of twitch chat users.

Unbiased
